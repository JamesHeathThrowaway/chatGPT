Common Birds in Your Backyard

American Robin

The American Robin is a friendly and familiar sight in backyards across North America. Easily recognizable by its bright red breast and dark gray head and back, robins are known for their cheerful songs.

Northern Cardinal

With its vibrant red plumage for males and warm brown feathers for females, the Northern Cardinal is a stunning bird. Cardinals are known for their loud, clear whistles and their habit of feeding at bird feeders.

The Blue Jay is a bold and intelligent bird with a striking blue body, white chest, and black crest. Blue Jays are known for their loud, harsh calls and their acrobatic flying skills.
